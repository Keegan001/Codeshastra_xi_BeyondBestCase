6-Day Maldives Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Malé & Resort Transfer
Arrive at Velana International Airport (MLE) in Malé.
Clear immigration and customs.
Transfer to your chosen resort island via speedboat or seaplane.
Check in to your resort and enjoy the afternoon at leisure.
Evening: Enjoy a delicious dinner at your resort.

Day 2: Resort Relaxation & Water Activities
Morning: Relax on the beach, swim in the crystal-clear waters, or enjoy the resort's amenities.
Afternoon: Choose from a variety of water activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, jet skiing, or paddleboarding.
Evening: Enjoy a beautiful sunset and dinner at your resort.

Day 3: Island Hopping & Local Culture
Morning: Take a boat trip to a nearby local island to experience Maldivian culture and interact with locals.
Afternoon: Explore the island, visit local shops, and enjoy lunch at a local restaurant.
Evening: Return to your resort and enjoy a relaxing evening.

Day 4: Marine Life Excursion
Morning: Embark on a snorkeling or diving excursion to explore the vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life.
Afternoon: Consider a dolphin or whale watching tour for a chance to see these amazing creatures in their natural habitat.
Evening: Enjoy a special dinner at your resort, perhaps a beach barbecue or a romantic meal under the stars.

Day 5: Spa & Wellness
Morning: Indulge in a relaxing spa treatment at your resort.
Afternoon: Spend the day at leisure, enjoying the resort's facilities, such as the pool, beach, or library.
Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at your resort.

Day 6: Departure
Enjoy a final breakfast at your resort.
Transfer back to Velana International Airport (MLE) for your departure.

Additional Suggestions:
Resort Options: Consider staying at resorts like the Four Seasons Landaa Giraavaru, The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort, or a more budget-friendly option like Adaaran Club Rannalhi.
Dining: Many resorts offer themed dinner nights (e.g., seafood BBQ, Maldivian cuisine). Check your resort's schedule.
Activities: Book popular activities like snorkeling or diving in advance.
Transportation: Confirm transfer details with your resort well in advance of your arrival.
Weather: The Maldives generally has warm, sunny weather. Pack light, breathable clothing, swimwear, and sunscreen.



6-Day Dubai Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Dubai Marina Exploration
Arrive at Dubai International Airport (DXB).
Check in to your hotel.
In the evening, explore Dubai Marina. Enjoy a walk along the promenade, admire the skyscrapers, and consider a dhow cruise.
Dinner at a restaurant in Dubai Marina.

Day 2: Downtown Dubai and Iconic Landmarks
Visit Downtown Dubai.
See the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building. You can go to the observation deck ("At the Top").
Visit the Dubai Mall for shopping and entertainment.
Watch the Dubai Fountain show in the evening.

Day 3: Desert Safari Adventure
Experience a desert safari. This typically includes dune bashing, a visit to a Bedouin-style camp, camel riding, and sandboarding.
Enjoy a BBQ dinner at the desert camp with traditional entertainment.

Day 4: Cultural Dubai and Souks
Visit the Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood to learn about Dubai's history.
Take an abra (traditional boat) ride across Dubai Creek.
Explore the traditional souks, such as the Gold Souk and the Spice Souk.

Day 5: Theme Parks or Relaxation
Choose between:
Option 1: Visit a theme park: IMG Worlds of Adventure, Dubai Parks and Resorts (Motiongate, Bollywood Parks, Legoland), or Wild Wadi Waterpark.
Option 2: Relax at one of Dubai's beaches (Jumeirah Beach, Kite Beach) or enjoy the hotel's amenities.
Evening: Enjoy dinner at a restaurant of your choice.

Day 6: Departure
Enjoy a final breakfast in Dubai.
Do some last-minute shopping or visit any attractions you missed.
Transfer to Dubai International Airport (DXB) for your departure.

Additional Suggestions:
Hotel Options: Consider staying in areas like Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, or Jumeirah Beach, depending on your preferences.
Dining: Dubai has a wide range of dining options, from traditional Emirati cuisine to international fine dining.
Activities: Book popular activities and attractions in advance, especially during peak season.
Transportation: Dubai has a good public transportation system, including the metro, buses, and taxis. Ride-hailing services are also readily available.
Weather: Dubai has a hot and dry climate. Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites or traditional areas.



6-Day Japan Itinerary

This itinerary focuses on the classic route for first-time visitors, covering Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka.

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo
Arrive at Narita (NRT) or Haneda (HND) Airport in Tokyo.
Transfer to your hotel in Tokyo.
Explore a local neighborhood in the evening (e.g., Shinjuku, Shibuya).
Dinner in Tokyo.

Day 2: Tokyo Exploration
Visit the Tsukiji Outer Market for a seafood breakfast and market experience.
Explore the Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, Tokyo's oldest temple.
Wander through the vibrant Akihabara district (electronics and anime).
In the evening, experience the bustling Shibuya crossing.
Dinner in Tokyo.

Day 3: Tokyo to Kyoto
Take the Shinkansen (bullet train) from Tokyo to Kyoto.
Check in to your hotel in Kyoto.
Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine with its thousands of red torii gates.
Explore Gion, Kyoto's geisha district, in the evening.
Dinner in Kyoto.

Day 4: Kyoto's Cultural Highlights
Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), a Zen temple covered in gold leaf.
Explore Arashiyama Bamboo Grove.
Visit Kiyomizu-dera Temple, known for its wooden stage and views.
Dinner in Kyoto.

Day 5: Kyoto to Osaka
Travel from Kyoto to Osaka (short train ride).
Visit Osaka Castle.
Explore Dotonbori, Osaka's vibrant entertainment and food district.
Enjoy Osaka's street food (takoyaki, okonomiyaki).
Dinner in Osaka.

Day 6: Departure
Depart from Osaka's Kansai International Airport (KIX).



6-Day Bali Itinerary

This itinerary offers a blend of cultural immersion, scenic beauty, and relaxation in Bali.

Day 1: Arrival in Bali & Uluwatu
Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS) in Denpasar.
Check in to your hotel in the Uluwatu area.
In the late afternoon, visit Uluwatu Temple, perched on a cliff overlooking the ocean.
Enjoy a Kecak fire dance performance at Uluwatu Temple.
Dinner at Jimbaran Bay for fresh seafood.

Day 2: Ubud's Cultural Heart
Travel to Ubud, the cultural center of Bali.
Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary.
Explore the Tegalalang Rice Terraces.
Visit Ubud Art Market for local handicrafts.
Dinner in Ubud, known for its diverse culinary scene.

Day 3: Kintamani & Volcanic Views
Visit the Kintamani region.
Enjoy panoramic views of Mount Batur and Lake Batur.
Optional: Visit a coffee plantation to learn about Balinese coffee.
Explore Tirta Empul Temple, a significant water temple.
Dinner in Ubud.

Day 4: Waterfalls & Nature
Visit one of Bali's stunning waterfalls, such as Tegenungan or Sekumpul.
Explore the natural beauty of the surrounding areas.
Optional: Enjoy a spa treatment in Ubud.
Dinner in Ubud.

Day 5: Beach Relaxation or Nusa Penida
Choose between:
Option 1: Relax on the beaches of Seminyak or Canggu. Enjoy the surf, sand, and beach clubs.
Option 2: Take a day trip to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach (T-Rex Bay), Angel's Billabong, and Broken Beach.
Dinner in Seminyak or Canggu.

Day 6: Departure
Enjoy a final breakfast in Bali.
Do some last-minute souvenir shopping.
Depart from Ngurah Rai International Airport (DPS).



6-Day Italy Itinerary

This itinerary focuses on the classic Italian cities of Rome, Florence, and Venice, offering a mix of history, art, and culture.

Day 1: Arrival in Rome
Arrive at Rome's Leonardo da Vinci-Fiumicino Airport (FCO).
Transfer to your hotel in Rome.
Depending on your arrival time, you can start exploring some of Rome's iconic sights:
The Colosseum
The Roman Forum
The Pantheon
Enjoy dinner in the Trastevere neighborhood, known for its lively atmosphere and traditional Roman cuisine.

Day 2: Ancient Rome and Vatican City
Visit the Vatican City:
St. Peter's Basilica
Vatican Museums
Sistine Chapel
Explore more of ancient Rome:
The Colosseum (if you didn't see it on Day 1)
The Roman Forum
Palatine Hill
In the evening, toss a coin in the Trevi Fountain and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of Piazza Navona.
Dinner in Rome.

Day 3: High-Speed Train to Florence
Take a morning high-speed train from Rome to Florence.
Check in to your hotel in Florence.
Visit the Accademia Gallery to see Michelangelo's David.
Explore the Florence Cathedral (Duomo) and climb Brunelleschi's Dome for panoramic views of the city.
Walk across the Ponte Vecchio, Florence's only bridge spared from destruction during World War II.
Dinner in Florence, and try some Florentine steak.

Day 4: Florence's Art and Oltrarno
Visit the Uffizi Gallery, one of the world's most important art museums, and see masterpieces by Botticelli, Leonardo da Vinci, and Michelangelo.
Explore the Oltrarno neighborhood, known for its artisan workshops and the Pitti Palace.
Visit the Boboli Gardens, behind the Pitti Palace, for a relaxing stroll.
Enjoy a gelato and explore Florence's charming streets.
Dinner in Florence.

Day 5: High-Speed Train to Venice
Take a morning high-speed train from Florence to Venice.
Check in to your hotel in Venice.
Visit St. Mark's Square:
St. Mark's Basilica
Doge's Palace
Climb St. Mark's Campanile for stunning views of Venice.
Take a gondola ride through the canals of Venice.
Dinner in Venice.

Day 6: Departure from Venice
Enjoy a final breakfast in Venice.
Depending on your flight schedule, you may have time for some last-minute sightseeing or souvenir shopping.
Depart from Venice's Marco Polo Airport (VCE).
